Introduction

Tian'anmen Square, located at the south end of the Forbidden City in the center of Beijing, the capital of the People's Republic of China, attracts worldwide attention for its outstanding architectural art and special political status.

Founding Ceremony

On October 1, 1949, the founding ceremony of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, namely the founding ceremony, was held grandly in Tian 'anmen Square in Beijing. The Central People's Government Committee, elected by the first plenary session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), held its first meeting at 2:00 PM in the Hall of Qinzheng.

In the cheers of the crowd, Mao Zedong solemnly declared to the world in his booming voice with a Hunan accent: "The Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China has been established today!" "Immediately, Mao Zedong read the No. 1 Proclamation of the Central People's Government to the world, followed by a grand military parade.
